Scene Manager

Login now with your Google account and upload your ADF-Files to easily share them between your devices.

1 Record a scene

Record an ADF-File with your Tango enabled device. Save and export it to the file system and you are ready to upload it.

2 Upload

To share your recorded ADF file, you can upload it to the scene manager out of the SceneManagerClient from the Play Store. Just select upload, authenticate with your Google account and upload the file.

3 Download

Other users can use ur ADF file and download it to use it for their applications. Just press the download button in SceneManagerClient. Its simple as that!
